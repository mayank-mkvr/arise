About ARISE

ARISE is a student-focused fitness tracker designed to help you stay healthy, productive, and focused. We combine AI with a user-friendly system to make your fitness journey smarter and easier.

Our mission is to help every student track their activity through voice commands, gamify progress, and level up their health.
